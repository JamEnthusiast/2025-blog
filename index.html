Social Links in Real Life: Why Making Friends Doesn't Unlock Cool Powers

(P.S. This blog is solely made for fun and realizations I’ve had after playing the game.)

Published on: March 11, 2025

Persona 4 Golden is an exciting, unique, and wholesome JRPG with thrilling mysteries. Your role as a player is to uncover these mysteries and reach out to the truth! With the help of your friends—I mean NPC friends—clearing the fog will not be as difficult as it may seem since these "Friends" are NPCs you can converse with. What can you gain from these interactions? Literal superpowers, you helped a brown-haired student from being inconveniently stuck inside a trash can? Boom, you can now summon wind ninja warrior... Or maybe you helped a troubled classmate by wearing disguised glasses? Nice! You now have the ability to spread flames and terror to your enemies by summoning a hybrid bird.

Meanwhile, in real life, making friends mostly just unlocks group chats that will either last for only a school year or an undying source of news, unpaid emotional labor, and the occasional free meal if you're lucky. So why is it that in the world of Persona 4, friendship fuels your ultimate power, but in reality, it mostly merely gives you someone to share memes with at 2 AM?
