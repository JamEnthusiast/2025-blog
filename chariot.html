The Chariot Arcana

(Persona 4 Golden / Tarot Arcana)

Reason#3: A Shadow of Your Former Self

Do you know that you are able to form a maximum of 24 social links during your entire playthrough? Numbers might seem small for our extroverted friends but for us NEETS JRPG fans, these are already equivalent to a crowd. Some might also argue that having few friends is better, Benjamin Franklin supports this logic with a quote in which he stated, “Be civil to all; serviceable to many; familiar with few; friend to one; enemy to none.”, although this makes sense, forming a lot of social links in Persona 4 will pay off in the long run.

Perks of maintaining a handful of social links in the game:

  • More social links mean more S-tier summons
  • Maxing all social links will renown you as the “Legend of Inaba”
  • To cope with the indescribable yearning of having friends
  • The ending will violently cut more onions for every social link you formed

On the other hand, all we get is:

  • Spend thousands of hours together only to realize your values don’t align with theirs (least unlucky result)
  • A best friend who says the most undeniable roast casually with a straight face (most lucky result)
  • Neverending guilt trip for something they did for you a long time ago that was supposed to make you feel indebted; yes it is the “Remember when—” line. (mid-tier result)
  • Refer to reason no. 3
